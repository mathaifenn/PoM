The Setting

See Fenn Hall on Google Maps

The workshop unfolds at Fenn Hall, a heritage home built in the early 1940s in traditional Kerala style. Its wooden beams, wide verandahs, and lotus pond create a space where ideas breathe easily. Four air-conditioned rooms are available, each providing accommodation for up to three participants. Guests are served simple, authentic Kerala meals that reflect the warmth of the place. More than a venue, Fenn Hall is part of the experience—its heritage and hospitality encouraging reflection, dialogue, and moments of clarity throughout the workshop.
