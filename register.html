How to Register

Date: 28th - 29th September 2025

Taking part couldn’t be easier. Begin with the quick Pre-Registration Form and share your interest. We’ll then bring together a small circle of participants. If you’re chosen, you’ll receive a personal email with details on how to confirm your place.

  1. Pre-Register – Fill out the form to express interest.
  2. Complete Registration – Selected participants will be invited to finalize their place.

Seats are limited and by invitation only. Pre‑Register on Google Forms
